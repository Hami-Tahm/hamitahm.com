## 1. Quick layout review

**Critical constraint:** Existing live sections are preserved. Only the six additions and minor checks below.

**The "near me" discipline:** Never write the literal phrase "near me" in copy — it reads as robotic and weakens the page. Google interprets `near me` queries through location signals (Toronto + Canada + `areaServed` schema). The job of this update is to make those signals dense and unambiguous, not to keyword-stuff.

**The six changes, in priority order:**

1. **HomeCalc proof block with Toronto-based framing** — adds local-authority signal that competitors can't match (dNOVO has no public case study; Chief AI Advisors is BC, not Toronto).
2. **ProfessionalService schema with `areaServed` + `priceRange`** — required for `near me` interpretation by Google.
3. **Pricing section** — `$1,500 flat` is the "affordable" keyword absorbed without apology. Hero the price.
4. **B2B section** — absorbs `ai visibility optimization consultant b2b`.
5. **Perplexity explicit mention** — absorbs `perplexity ai visibility agency` by covering Perplexity prominently while never claiming "agency."
6. **First paragraph + Toronto density throughout** — Toronto must appear 3–4 times across the page.

**Plus:** title + meta + FAQ additions + minor checks.

**Out of scope but currently blocking local-pack visibility:** Google Business Profile setup. The primary KW is a `near me` query — without GBP, Hami is structurally invisible in the Google local pack regardless of how good the on-page signals are. dNOVO Group (Toronto) and Chief AI Advisors (Victoria BC) both have GBP. Recommend running GBP setup in parallel with this deploy.

**What not to touch:** existing visual layout, navigation, footer, H1 if it already includes "Canada."
