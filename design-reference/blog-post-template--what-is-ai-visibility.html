What Is AI Visibility?

As people stop Googling and start asking AI, being named in the answer becomes the new being found. Here's what that means — in plain English.

Hami Tahm
Updated 2026 · 6 min read
In short

AI visibility is how often, and how favourably, your business appears in answers from AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews. It's the AI-era successor to SEO: instead of ranking a link, you're trying to be the source the AI names and cites.

For twenty years, "being found online" meant one thing: ranking on Google. You optimized a page, climbed the results, and hoped people clicked. That game still exists — but a second game has started next to it, and most businesses haven't noticed.

People are increasingly skipping the list of links entirely. They ask ChatGPT "who's a good mortgage broker in Toronto?" They type a question into Perplexity. They read the AI Overview at the top of Google and never scroll. In each case, an AI gives them an answer that names a few businesses — and silently leaves out everyone else.

AI visibility is simply: are you one of the names the AI mentions?

Why this is different from SEO

It's tempting to think this is just SEO with a new label. It isn't, though the foundations overlap. The difference is in the target:

  • SEO optimizes to rank a link in a list. The user still chooses and clicks.
  • AI visibility optimizes to be selected and cited inside a generated answer. The AI chooses for the user.

That shift changes what matters. Answer engines lean heavily on how clearly your content can be extracted, how well your brand is understood as a distinct entity, and how much third-party authority backs you up. In fact, research on Google AI Overviews has found that a meaningful share of cited sources don't even rank on the first page of normal results — which means the old playbook doesn't automatically carry over.

You can rank #1 on Google and still be invisible in AI answers. They're related games, not the same game.

The three terms you'll hear

This field is young, so the vocabulary is still settling. Three acronyms come up constantly:

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization

Structuring your content so answer engines can cleanly extract and cite it. Think clear questions and direct answers, not buried paragraphs.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

The broader practice of earning mentions and citations inside AI-generated responses — heavily influenced by authority and what others say about you, not just your own pages.

AI SEO / AI visibility

The umbrella term. If the acronyms feel confusing, that's normal — most buyers just want one thing: "I want to show up when people ask AI about what I do." I break down how AEO, GEO and SEO differ here.

Why it matters now, not later

AI search is newer and far less saturated than Google. The businesses that show up well in AI answers today are mostly there by luck — very few are optimizing for it deliberately. That's the opening. Moving early in an uncrowded channel is how you win before it becomes competitive.

If you want to understand the practical side next, read how to check your AI visibility, or how to get your business mentioned by ChatGPT.

The short version: search is splitting into two games, and a new one is wide open. AI visibility is making sure that when the machine answers, your name is in it.
