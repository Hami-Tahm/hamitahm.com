06 — Questions

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility is how often — and how favourably — your business appears in answers from AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews. As people shift from searching to asking, being named in those answers becomes as important as ranking on Google ever was.

How is this different from regular SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranked lists of links. AI visibility optimizes for being chosen and cited inside a generated answer, which depends more on entity clarity, structured content, and third-party authority. The skills overlap, but the target is different — and answer engines frequently cite sources that don't rank on page one.

Do you only work with Canadian businesses?

I'm based in Toronto and most of my clients are Canadian — clinics, real-estate and mortgage brands, and SaaS companies. I take select clients elsewhere, but Canadian and North American businesses are the core focus.

How do we start?

With a free AI Visibility Snapshot to see where you stand. If it makes sense to go deeper, we move to a paid audit, then a fixed-scope implementation sprint — no open-ended retainers unless you want ongoing work. Start with the snapshot here.

How long until I see results?

It depends on your starting point and competition, but AI search is newer and less saturated than Google, so well-executed work can show up faster than traditional SEO. The audit gives you a realistic timeline for your specific situation.
